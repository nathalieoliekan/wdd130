Overview

Purpose

Welcome to Whitewater Rafting LLC! As the proud owner of this exhilarating adventure company, I am thrilled to announce our leap into the digital realm. Our decision to establish an online presence stems from a deep commitment to providing everyone the change to get on the water and experience the great outdoors in a whole new way! We are a gateway to unforgettable adventures!.

Audience

At Whitewater Rafting LLC, we believe that everyone deserves a taste of adventure, and our online platform allows us to extend that opportunity far beyond the confines of our physical location. Whether you're a seasoned rafter or just starting out, our website is designed to inspire, inform, and empower you every step of the way. Join us as we explore new paths and create exceptional memories! Q1: Who are the target customers? A1: The target audience is anyone who wants to river raft. No limit on age except that they have to be old enough to swim, and in moderate health. Q2: What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? A2: They should expect to find information about where to schedule rafting trips, how to prepare, and cool activities they can do. Q3: How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? A3: My audience will use all devices to access the site, but mostly mobile phones as these are easily portable for those who go on adventures.
